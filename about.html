WELCOME TO ORCHIDS

Orchids The International School is one of India's leading chains of CBSE and ICSE schools, with 90+ schools across the country. Orchids found their first home in Hyderabad. Since then, we have spread our wings across 25+ cities and still grwoing.

We are redefining education for upcoming generations with smart classes, a remodeled educational philosophy, top-notch infrastructure, and a technology-integrated curriculum. Such innovation has brought us recognition from Times School Survey Awards and Asian Education Leadership Awards.

Vision

ORCHIDS aims to create a dynamic future generation that can imagine and innovate their own revolutions.

Mission

With the world changing constantly, Orchids prepare students to adapt and excel through an inspiring and challenging environment. ORCHIDS aims at the student's holistic development in the most exciting way.

5 Unique Pillars

Academic Excellence

Innovative teaching methods to nurture the potentials of the students, driving them towards achieving academic excellence.

Academic Excellence

Orchids The International School is renowned for turning learning into a fun experience. In spite of just focusing on theory and grades, our teachers make sure that kids are happy and understand the foundational concepts. We integrate technology with the curriculum so our students can match the ever-evolving world. Our optimal digital classrooms are equipped to enhance the learning experience with digital boards, 3D animated videos, innovative tools, and unique assessments to make the classroom fun. Orchids design engaging academic experiences that include robotics, financial literacy, public speaking, astronomy, etc. Our Central Academic & Research Team (CART), composed of highly qualified and experienced academicians, provides curriculum, training, and support to all our schools across India to ensure standardization. Students discover their interests, talents, and the joy of learning through experiences inside the classroom, STEM labs, and across the campus.

Personal Attention

A team of Skilled and experienced teachers who are experts in their respective fields to cater to the curiosities of each students.

Personal Attention

If you are your child's teacher at home, we are the parents at school. Our well-trained teachers are responsible for the academic as well as personal care of the child, ensuring that the child meets challenges with courage and a problem-solving attitude. Their support gives students confidence and teaches them how to be independent. Our young students are always accompanied by caretakers and coordinators during indoor and outdoor activities at school. We focus on teaching values, identifying students' interests, and guiding them to grow in the desired direction.

Holistic Development

Learning beyond classrooms through a spectrum of extra-curricular activities for an all round progress.

Holistic Development

We prepare students at every age to be successful and happy during their time at Orchids, universities, and beyond... Children grow with challenging academics, competitive and innovative programs, first-class facilities, and top-notch faculty to meet parents' expectations. Learning beyond classrooms through a spectrum of extra-curricular activities for overall progress. We provide a hands-on learning experience through science, Mathematics, and robotics learning kits to shape their imagination. To give them a glimpse of the real-world scenario, we have frequent field trips along with summer camps to develop the learn-by-doing attitude and decision-making skills. Grooming a well-rounded personality by taking measures like Health & Nutrition Program, training in Yoga & Meditation, Taekwondo, and Sports for physical fitness and overall well-being. We even provide a diet chart as per their BMI. Along with academics, our beloved students are also encouraged to be involved in sports activities and performing arts, to participate and perform in various inter-school, state-level, national and international competitions.

Best-in-class infrastructure

World-class infrastructure to complement the academic process and extra-curricular activities.

Best-in-class Infrastructure

Orchids The International School is facilitated by a world-class infrastructure for academic and co-curricular activities. As practical learning is on top priority, we have labs for science, computers, robotics, and a planetarium which are vital assets to impart a sense of exploration and creativity among the kids. For our students' physical and mental endurance, we have expansive sports facilities with a cricket ground, basketball court, and swimming pool. At Orchids, we have activity rooms and a theater for plays, dramas, dancing, and singing, as there is a greater importance to soft skills, which play a vital role in personality development.
