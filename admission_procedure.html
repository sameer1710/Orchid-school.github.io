Orchids Brochure

Orchids The International School is extremely passionate to achieve academic excellence through playful and fun learning. At Orchids we focus on holistic development as our curriculum is backed by programmes such as public speaking, astronomy, taekwondo, skating and swimming pool with best-in-class Infrastructure. Our well certified teachers and trained coordinators are always accompanied with kids to help and guide them. We have a Safe Campus with 24X7 CCTV Surveillance and security guards.

EYP Brochure

Our EYP covers pre-nursery to grade 2. At this preparatory stage, we focus on developing language and numeracy skills, with emphasis on cognitive skills and intelligence. At Orchids kids are encouraged to participate in art and craft, music and dance which leads to overall growth and development of kids.

Contact Us

Parents fill out the Admission Enquiry Form on our website, choosing the branch of their preference. Upon submission of the Admission Enquiry Form, the parents receive communication regarding their location and contact details for the next steps.Feel free to contact our admission cell for any inquiries regarding your child's admission at ORCHIDS.

Campus Visit

Our school counselor will connect with the parent over call and share information about the school. fee structure, virtual counseling or phone counseling, campus visit, etc.

Application Form

The parents are then requested to purchase the Admission/Application Kit worth Rs. 200 from the School Admission Application Form. To proceed further, parents will submit the Application Form for their childs school admission with all necessary documents. If the admission is for Grade 6 and above, the child has to undergo an online Baseline Assessment Test.Parents can confirm the admission by paying Rs. 11,300/- (Registration amount - Rs.1300/- and Admission Blocking amount - Rs. 10,000/-).

3 passport size photographs-father & mother/ guardian
Declaration form
AADHAR CARD

Marks* statement copy

Enrollment Number

Parents can acquire their childs enrollment number, and the admission process successfully comes to an end.
