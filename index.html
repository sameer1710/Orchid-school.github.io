Orchids found its first home in Hyderabad. Since then, we have spread our wings across 25+ cities.

We are redefining the education of upcoming generations with smart classes, remodeled educational philosophy and top-notch infrastructure. Such innovation has brought us recognitions from Times School Survey Awards and Asian Education Leadership Awards.
